FAQ

Q. How is my score calculated?

A. Your score is based on the amount of tasks you completed from your total tasks for that day, and the priority or weighting of the task.


Q. How can I get the best score?

A. The best way to get a higher score is to make sure you get your tasks done by the end of the day.


Q. What if I forget to input whether or not I did my tasks at the end of the day?

A. You are able to fill out the checklist the next day if you forget. You will have to complete it before you can start the next day.


Q. Can I add new tasks throughout the day?

A. Yes, you can add in new tasks anywhere you have time that you haven't assigned any tasks to.


Q. Can I change my schedule?

A. You can edit each task and move it to a new time slot, or swap it with another task.

How To Use

To use the Productivity Calculator, follow these steps:

1. At the start of each day, upload all of your tasks or things you want to get done for the day. Go to the create schedule page and fill out the table. If you don't have a task for a certain period, leave it blank.

2. Save the schedule by clicking confirm. This will lock in your schedule. If you want to view it at any time during the day, click load shedule to load the schedule you made for that day.

3. At the end of the day, click on the end day button and you will ba taken to a checklist where you check off the tasks that you completed during the day. Make sure to be honest about which ones you completed and which ones you did not complete. When you have finished, click the calculate score button, and your score for that day will be calculated and displayed.
